About Simply Macarons

Simply Macarons is a home-based macaron bakery in Victoria, BC specializing in handmade, small-batch macarons with big flavour and beautiful designs.

Founded by Alex, Simply Macarons started with a passion for precision baking and a love of sharing joy through sweet treats. Each macaron is lovingly crafted with high-quality ingredients, vibrant colours, and a perfect balance of texture and flavour.
